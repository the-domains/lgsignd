### Handmade knitted**Ladybird **doll wearing removable dress. The eyes and the mouth are embroidered.

* **Height approx.** 14.96 inches (38 cm)
* **Yarn material :**

* **Stuffing: **100% polyester fiber
* **Available colors:**
  1. red
  2. dark red
